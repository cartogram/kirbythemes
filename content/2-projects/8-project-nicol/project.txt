Text: For David R. Harper’s Entre le chien et le loup, careful design choices suggest the artist’s influences upon his practice through the use of special materials. Laser-cut stars, referencing constellations, embellish the book’s slipcase. Signature shades of blue, suggesting a twilight sky (neither dawn nor dusk), appear throughout the catalogue—most prominently in the texts and the star-filled endpapers. Foil stamping and a powerful 10-page roll-fold insert complete the range of materials and processes selected to enhance this publication.

David R. Harper
Entre le chien et le loup
The Doris McCarthy Gallery, University of Toronto Scarborough to.
